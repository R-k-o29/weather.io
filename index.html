About Weather.io
Weather.io is a web application that provides accurate and up-to-date weather information for cities around the world. Get real-time temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more with our user-friendly interface.
Usage Guide
To use Weather.io, simply enter the name of your city in the search bar and click the "Search" button. The application will display the current weather details for the specified city. You can also view weather information for prominent cities listed on the homepage.
